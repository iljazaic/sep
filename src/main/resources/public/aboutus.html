About Us

Cloverville is an eco-village with 42 residents led by Green Bob. The community is built around a shared commitment to sustainable living, where residents participate in environmentally friendly “green activities” and communal tasks. These activities support Cloverville’s goal of documenting, managing, and motivating sustainable behaviour across the village. To encourage participation and track contributions, Cloverville uses a point-based system that reflects both personal and community-level involvement in sustainability efforts. Residents can earn points through individual green actions, shared tasks, and local trade offers, creating a transparent and community-driven approach to environmental engagement.
